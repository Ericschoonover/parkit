# OPERATING AGREEMENT
# OF
# PARKIT LLC
# (A Virginia Limited Liability Company)

---

**This Operating Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of ____________, 2026, by the following Member:

**Member:** Eric Schoonover ("Member")

---

## ARTICLE I — FORMATION

1.1 **Formation.** The Member hereby forms a limited liability company ("Company") pursuant to the Virginia Limited Liability Company Act, Va. Code Ann. §§ 13.1-1000 et seq. (the "Act"). The Company shall be known as "ParkIt LLC" (or such other name as may be filed with the Virginia State Corporation Commission).

1.2 **Filing.** The Articles of Organization shall be filed with the Virginia State Corporation Commission ("SCC"). The Company shall exist from the date of filing until dissolved in accordance with this Agreement or the Act.

1.3 **Registered Agent.** The Company's registered agent and registered office in Virginia shall be as designated in the Articles of Organization and updated as required by the Act.

1.4 **Principal Office.** The principal office of the Company shall be located at such address as the Member may designate from time to time.

---

## ARTICLE II — PURPOSE

2.1 **Purpose.** The Company is organized for the purpose of operating an online marketplace platform that connects parking space owners with individuals seeking parking, and any other lawful business activity for which a limited liability company may be organized under the Act.

2.2 **Powers.** The Company shall have all the powers granted to limited liability companies under the Act.

---

## ARTICLE III — MEMBER

3.1 **Sole Member.** The sole member of the Company is Eric Schoonover.

3.2 **Interest.** The Member owns 100% of the membership interests in the Company.

---

## ARTICLE IV — CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

4.1 **Initial Contribution.** The Member shall make an initial capital contribution to the Company in the amount of $__________ on or before __________, 2026.

4.2 **Additional Contributions.** The Member may make additional capital contributions at any time. The Member is not obligated to make any additional contributions.

4.3 **No Interest on Contributions.** Capital contributions shall not bear interest.

---

## ARTICLE V — PROFITS, LOSSES, AND DISTRIBUTIONS

5.1 **Profits and Losses.** All profits, losses, deductions, and credits of the Company shall be allocated to and borne by the Member.

5.2 **Distributions.** The Member may cause the Company to distribute available funds to the Member at such times and in such amounts as the Member determines, subject to the Company's obligations and applicable law.

5.3 **Tax Treatment.** The Company is a disregarded entity for federal and state income tax purposes. All income, losses, deductions, and credits of the Company shall be reported on the Member's personal income tax returns.

---

## ARTICLE VI — MANAGEMENT

6.1 **Member-Managed.** The Company shall be managed by the Member (a "member-managed" LLC under the Act). The Member has full authority to manage the business and affairs of the Company.

6.2 **Powers of the Member.** The Member may, on behalf of the Company:
- Enter into contracts and agreements
- Open and manage bank accounts
- Hire employees, contractors, and agents
- Acquire, hold, and dispose of property
- Borrow money and incur debt
- File tax returns and make tax elections
- Settle claims and litigation
- Do all other acts necessary or appropriate to carry out the Company's business

6.3 **No Officers Required.** The Member may, but is not required to, appoint officers or agents of the Company.

6.4 **Delegation.** The Member may delegate authority to employees, contractors, or agents as the Member sees fit.

---

## ARTICLE VII — BANKING AND RECORDS

7.1 **Bank Accounts.** The Company shall maintain one or more separate bank accounts in the name of the Company. No Company funds shall be commingled with the personal funds of the Member.

7.2 **Financial Records.** The Company shall maintain accurate and complete financial records, including:
- Bank statements and records of all deposits and withdrawals
- Records of all income and expenses
- Tax returns and supporting documentation
- This Operating Agreement and any amendments

7.3 **Annual Review.** The Member shall review the Company's financial records at least annually.

---

## ARTICLE VIII — LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

8.1 **Limited Liability.** Except as otherwise required by the Act or this Agreement, the Member shall not be personally liable for any debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company, whether arising in contract, tort, or otherwise, solely by reason of being a member of the Company.

8.2 **Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Member from and against any and all claims, liabilities, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from or related to the business of the Company, except to the extent arising from the Member's:
- Gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Breach of this Agreement
- Violation of applicable law

8.3 **Veil Protection.** To maintain the limited liability protection of the Company, the Member shall:
- Maintain separate bank accounts for the Company
- Not commingle Company funds with personal funds
- Maintain adequate capitalization of the Company
- Conduct Company business in the Company's name
- Keep Company records and filings current
- Maintain this Operating Agreement as a written record

---

## ARTICLE IX — TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTEREST

9.1 **Transfer.** The Member may transfer, assign, or dispose of all or any portion of the Member's interest in the Company, subject to the Act.

9.2 **Admission of Additional Members.** No person shall be admitted as a member of the Company without the written consent of the Member.

---

## ARTICLE X — DISSOLUTION

10.1 **Events of Dissolution.** The Company shall be dissolved upon the occurrence of any of the following:
- The written decision of the Member
- The entry of a decree of judicial dissolution under the Act
- The occurrence of any event that makes it unlawful to carry on the Company's business

10.2 **Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Company's affairs shall be wound up, its assets distributed, and its liabilities satisfied in accordance with the Act and this Agreement.

10.3 **Distributions on Dissolution.** After payment of all liabilities, remaining assets shall be distributed to the Member.

---

## ARTICLE XI — GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

11.2 **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by the Member.

11.3 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

11.4 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement of the Member with respect to the Company and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral.

11.5 **Binding Effect.** This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the Member and the Member's heirs, executors, administrators, successors, and assigns.

11.6 **Headings.** The headings in this Agreement are for convenience only and shall not affect the interpretation of this Agreement.
